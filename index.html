The Challenge with Current Subsidy Systems

Manual and opaque disbursement processes are susceptible to delays and misuse

Subsidies are essential to catalyze the early growth of the green hydrogen sector, but traditional disbursement methods create significant challenges:

Delayed Payments

Manual verification processes lead to significant delays in subsidy disbursement

Lack of Transparency

Opaque processes make it difficult to track how funds are allocated and used

Fraud Risk

Manual systems are vulnerable to misappropriation and fraudulent claims

Administrative Burden

High administrative costs for verification and disbursement processes
